About

I've been developing software under various names and handles for a very long time. Under the BaconPaul handle, I work on various open source projects and musical explorations with collaborators interested in a friendly and open collaboration.
The best way to reach me is probably to open a relevant github issue on one of my projects or to find the various chat rooms associated with my projects and talk there. A soundcloud comment might work, but a twitter mention probably won't.
